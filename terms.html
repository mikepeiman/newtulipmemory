Terms & Conditions

Shipping

We charge USD $80 flat per shipment for Fedex next day delivery in Canada/USA. Other arrangements can be made.

Warranty

3 year parts and labour warranty for original purchaser; customer pays for return shipping; we pay for shipping to customer.

Installation Support

Phone and/or Skype support is free to assist with your installation, if needed (detailed, step-by-step instructions are provided as well; support is not typically required).

Technical Support

Technical support for Tulip products is free of charge. If technical support is required for non-Tulip products, we are available at a competitive hourly rate.

Returns

Returned goods are tested upon receipt. For products tested defective, we retain the option to repair or replace. If a return is made due to a change of mind, a credit will be issued.
